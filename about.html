About Victory Investment Limited


Victory Oil Investment involves various focus areas namely Oil and Gas, Agriculture and Aviation. The Group is headquartered in Nigeria. The groups focus geographical area is Pan Africa with the special focus in Sub-Saharan West Africa. Ultimate group was established to manage all sister concerns of the rapidly expanding group of companies. Free to focus on strategic development and growth, Victory Investment is capitalizing on the market access afforded by Nigeria, as it plants roots deep throughout its trade routes and corporate networks. Victory Investment Limited is seeking to add value and enact economic development by meeting the needs of a developing Africa by offering international management and solutions to local problems. Overlooking the group's vast activities, managing change, forecasting progress and forging bonds on an international and government level.
